About us

Clay and wine started as a small studio ran by two cousins David and Željko Birić.

The story was born in 2021 when we launched our first collection online and we were
overwhelmed by the response of our audience. After that we continued to make several collections.

Our first studio, Studio Vinkovci wears the name of the location. Over the course of
three years we have built two more studios - one in Lički Osik which is our biggest studio and one in Zagreb which is our most visited studio.

The brand creates handmade ceramics, mostly mugs, with lots of love. Every single piece is meant to brighten every sip of coffee, wine or tea.
