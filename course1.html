12 week ceramics for beginners

The next course for total beginners starts on the 29th of January 2024. Classes run once a week, 3 school hours at a time.

Our courses are designed as oasis of peace, and are adult only. For English speaking individuals everything is translated to English.Complete course lasts 3 months (12 classes, 36 school hours in total). It is possible to pay for each month separately. The price for the complete course is reduced.

In the 1st month the emphasis are on hand modelling and building terracotta clay. Students make their own pieces and also learn about storing and drying clay.

In the 2nd month of the course, students are working and playing with white stoneware clay and more emphasis is given to decorating, embellishing, personalising clay through several different techniques.

In the 3rd month yet another type of clay is introduced and students also get a chance to glaze their own brand new ceramic pieces.

At the end of the 3rd month students are taking home their finished pieces, fired twice, glazed and ready for everyday use. Students attending all 12 weeks will personally experience all segments of ceramics' creation process.

Price of the course is 250.00€.
