Individual workshops

Book workshop for yourself or bring a neighbour, a friend, colleague, and do tell us if it is a gift or a special occasion you are celebrating by creating something unique in clay. Come alone or bring friends and or family. Organize team building with your colleagues or an afternoon out with your partner.

Our workshops last for 3 school hours (135 minutes). They are suitable for one to nine persons booking together, such as two or more friends, mother and daughter, sisters, girlfriend and boyfriend, work colleagues. You can book as few or as many workshops as you wish. Date for each workshop is agreed separately to suit all parties.

Use of all necessary tools, equipment, clays, glazes, special engobas, two kiln firings per workshop, in a ceramic kiln on temperatures of 1000ºC and tuition are all included in the price.

You will decorate your own hand modelled pieces you have made at previous workshop with special ceramic paints called engobas. Allow your imagination to take charge and free your hands to express themselves through drawing and painting clay. After drying, glazing and two firings you can collect your pieces. Alternatively we can ship your pieces to your home address at a fee.

Price of the course is 30.00€.
